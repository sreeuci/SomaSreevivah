The Couple

Long long ago (7 years), science played cupid to bring together a girl called Soma and a boy called Sree. Soma is from Bihar and is cheerful, outgoing and fun-loving while Sree is from Tamil Nadu and is serious and introverted who occasionally cracks bad jokes. They met in Kerala while pursuing their undergraduate studies. Apart from their personalities they have a very different upbringing as well in terms of culture, language, food, etc. Long story short Soma found a person who can listen to her talk non-stop and Sree found a person who puts a smile on his face. To forever experience the best of both worlds they obviously decided to get married. They hope to bring people with very diverse backgrounds together for their wedding which would be a concoction of north & south Indian culture.
